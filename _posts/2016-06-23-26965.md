<p>iTunes connectでアプリのバージョンアップを行ったのですが、以下のメッセージでリジェクトされました。何を修正したらよろしいのでしょうか。</p>

<blockquote>
  <p>Before you Submit</p>
  
  <p>Your app does not comply with the Guidelines for Using Apple's<br>
  Trademarks and Copyrights. Specifically, your app includes:</p>
  
  <p>The term iOS in the device name.</p>
  
  <p>Next Steps</p>
  
  <p>Please revise your app and its metadata to address these trademark<br>
  issues.</p>
  
  <p>For your convenience, we've provided the relevant section/s of the<br>
  Guidelines for Using Apple's Trademarks and Copyrights:</p>
  
  <p>Product Names Guidelines</p>
  
  <p>You may use Apple, iPhone, or any other Apple word mark (but not the<br>
  Apple Logo or other Apple-owned graphic symbol/logo) in a referential<br>
  phrase on packaging or promotional/advertising materials to describe<br>
  that the third party product is compatible with the referenced Apple<br>
  product or technology, provided they comply with the following<br>
  requirements:</p>
  
  <ul>
  <li>The Apple word mark is not part of the product name.</li>
  <li>The Apple word mark is used in a referential phrase such as "runs on", "for use with", "for", or "compatible with".</li>
  <li>The Apple word mark appears less prominent than the product name.</li>
  <li>The product is in fact compatible with, or otherwise works with, the referenced Apple product.</li>
  <li>The reference to Apple does not create a sense of endorsement, sponsorship, or false association with Apple or Apple products or<br>
  services.</li>
  <li>The use does not show Apple or its products in a false or derogatory light.</li>
  </ul>
  
  <p>In other words, whether appearing on the application itself and/or in<br>
  marketing materials, the word iPhone may be used as a descriptor, but<br>
  not as part of the product name.</p>
</blockquote>
