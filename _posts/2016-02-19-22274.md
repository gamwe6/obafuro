<p>■test.sh</p>

<pre class="lang-sh prettyprint-override"><code>#! /bin/sh

myfol=`awk -F"/"  '{print $NF} ${1}`
mylog=2016_${myfol}.txt
touch ${mylog}
</code></pre>

<p>■実行時</p>

<pre><code>test.sh /var/log/aaa_sever
</code></pre>

<blockquote>
