<pre><code>listen "/tmp/unicorn_server.sock", backlog: 1024
</code></pre>

<p>nginxの設定</p>

<pre><code># unicorn

}

  listen       80;
  server_name  site-a.com;
  root         /var/www/site-a/public;

  location / {
    proxy_pass http://unicorn_server;
